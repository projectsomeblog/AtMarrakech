Must-See Attractions and Experiences

AtMarrakech emphasizes both iconic and off-the-beaten-path experiences to help visitors fully appreciate the city. Some featured highlights include:

  • Jemaa el-Fnaa: Marrakech’s central square, famous for its street performers, food stalls, and evening entertainment.
  • The Medina: A UNESCO World Heritage site with winding alleys, bustling souks, and historic landmarks.
  • Jardin Majorelle and YSL Museum: A peaceful oasis and artistic landmark offering a break from the city’s hustle.
  • Historic Palaces: Bahia and El Badi Palaces, showcasing Moroccan architecture, craftsmanship, and royal history.
